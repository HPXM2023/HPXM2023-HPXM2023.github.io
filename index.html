High Precision X-Ray Measurements 2023
Istituto Nazionale di Fisica Nucleare
19-23 June 2023 @ Frascati, Italy

High Precision X-ray Measurements 2023 conference is the third edition of the HPXM conference, held at the INFN Laboratories of Frascati in 2018 and 2021.

In the wake of the success of the 2018 and 2021 editions, HPXM2023 is planned with the twofold aim to consolidate the existing interconnections between the research teams and foster creating new ones, offering the opportunity for all the participants to discuss and share the results of their activities focusing on a common protagonist: X-ray precision detection.

The aim of this conference is to inform the participants on the most recent developments in X-ray detection technologies and their impacts in various sectors like nuclear physics, astrophysics, quantum physics, XRF, XES, EXAFS, PIXE, plasma emission spectroscopy, monochromators, synchrotron radiation, advanced statistical and machine learnign methods, radioprotection, telescopes and space engineering, medical applications, food and beverage quality control and elemental mapping.

The scientific program consists of invited lectures from distinguished scientists and oral presentations given by the participants; a technical and industrial session is also foreseen.

Main Topics of the conference will be: X-ray energy and position detectors, optics, imaging and tracing simulations, as well as their application in biology, astrophysics, nuclear physics, exotic atoms, medical physics and agrifood; spectrometers, graphite-based applications and chemical analysis.

Best Young Researchers presentation award: A best presentation contest will take place to honor the most inspiring talks presented by Ph.D students during the conference. Winners, elected by a commission to be selected among the conference participants, will be awarded prizes provided by the MDPI sponsor.
